About Mia's Pizzeria

Pizza Trivia

What is the origin of the popular pizza topping, "Pepperoni"?

Contrary to popular belief, the pepperoni topping, which has become a staple on pizzas around the world, does not have Italian origins. It actually originated in the United States.
